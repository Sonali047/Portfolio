EpochEdge, the main attraction of this project is Face Recognition System. Suppose you attend an event and click photos in camera of a friend or photographer. That friend or photographer will upload all photos to "Upload your image" section of EpochEdge website. You just need to open the EpochEdge website and go to "Get all session image" section to download all images or "Get your image" section to download only those images you are present through Face Scan.

Important Note: Photographer will upload the photos with a specific username called Session username and you must know that username to collect the images.
